Lion Events

September 2022 - present

Projects

When I arrived in Edinburgh, I realised the lack of representation of the Hispanic and Latin communities within the events scene. To fill the gap in the market, I joined two other share-holders to create "LatinFest". By hiring some of the best Latin talents, renting the most prestegious venues in Edinburgh, investing into outrageous productions and a LOT of work in marketing, I can produly say that LatinFest is now one of the biggest Latin events in Scotland. In the first year, we ran 8 events hosting 8000+ people in total. Lion events is the registered company we funded, under which we operate Latin Fest.

Within the company, I manage a team of 80+ promoters, negotiate with venues, societies and partners, analyse data on past events to predict trends and opportunities and make critical decisions based on them.
